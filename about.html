The Minds Behind AvsarSetu

A team of passionate first-year B.Tech students on a mission to revolutionize internship access for Smart India Hackathon.

Our Story

It all started with a shared vision for the Smart India Hackathon. As first-year engineering students, we saw a gap between talented students and meaningful government internships. We wanted to build a bridge—a 'Setu'—using the power of AI to connect aspiring minds with deserving opportunities. AvsarSetu is our solution, born from late-night coding sessions and a passion to make a real impact.
